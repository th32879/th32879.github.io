My Favorite Music

a project by: Taylor Hill

Introduction

Generally, I listen to most genres of music. Since my parents exposed me to so many types of music at such a young age, I now have a very diverse music taste. My dad listened to mainly jazz, classic rock, rap, reggae, and grunge. My mom listened to pop music, classic rock, and jazz. My parents both loved jazz because they grew up in New Orleans, where it is extremely popular. Between my parents' preferences as well as what I have grown to like on my own, I have been able to create an excellent, everchanging collection of music to listen to daily.

Pop

Pop music is one of my favorite genres. There is constantly pop music that is being released. However, pop music has become extremely mainstream; therefore, the songs are known by most. Pop can mean many different sounds. For example, there is pop music by artists such as Michael Jackson. Although, comparing it to more prominent artists of today such as Katy Perry or Justin Bieber proves that pop music, as well as music overall, is constantly changing and evolving. Many artists of all genres will dabble in pop music nowadays, so it is a highly-known and popular genre.

Jazz

Jazz in the United States originated in Louisiana, which is where my parents and I are from. Therefore, it is one of my favorite genres. My favorite artist overall is Amy Winehouse, however I also appreciate the music of Ella Fitzgerald and Louis Armstrong. Between you and I, Amy Winehouse will forever be the best. I love smooth but also sassy voices in jazz. Billie Holiday is one of my favorites as well. I love when the jazz music involves lots of trumpet and is upbeat.

Reggae

Although he is extremely well-known, Bob Marley would have to be my favorite reggae artist. Some people don't enjoy reggae because they think that it all sounds the same, but I enjoy it. Reggae is relaxing and peaceful to me. It puts me into a better mood. My favorite songs by Bob Marley are "Is This Love?" and "Buffalo Soldier." His sons, Damian and Stephen Marley, made a song called "Medication" that I like to listen to as well. I listened to reggae when I was on a cruise to the Bahamas, so now every time I hear it, I think of the Bahamas.

Trap

Trap music as well as rap music is one of my favorites as well. It is "hype" music that you can get lit to. It's best to listen to when you are on the way to go do something fun. The most popular artists in either trap or rap music currently are Migos, 21 Savage, Travi$ Scott, Cardi B, and Nicki Minaj. I personally love Cardi B and her hit song "Bodak Yellow" as well as her verse on the song "Motorsport," which also features Migos and Nicki Minaj. This type of music has slowly but surely began to dominant the charts. This is becoming an "everyone" type of music, meaning many types of people can listen to and like this music. It is becoming popular in celebrity culture as well.

Alternative Rock/Grunge

My like of alternative rock and grunge music has definitely developed thanks to my parents. They were big fans of bands such as Nirvana and Pearl Jam, and this transferred over to me. This is the type of musi that my dad and I listen to whenever we're in the car together. I enjoy Nirvana as well. My favorite songs by Nirvana are mostly on their album Nevermind . The lead singer of the band Nirvana quite unfortunately died in 1994, ending the band's career. However, Dave Grohl, the drummer, went on to be extremely successful in the music industry by starting his own bands, the most famous being "The Foo Fighters."

Classic Rock

Lastly, I am a huge fan of classic rock. This comes from my parents as well. They would always show me iconic songs from the 1970s and 80s (in terms of classic rock), and as a family, we would play a game on the PlayStation called Rockband . My favorite classic rock bands are Journey, Boston, Bon Jovi, and Queen. I would always love to sing a good rock ballad! My favorite classic rock song is "Bohemian Rhapsody," and it is very common in my family for all of us to join in singing this, even though no one can really hit the notes of Freddie Mercury like he can.
